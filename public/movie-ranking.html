The Best Movies According to Finn

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Die Hard

This is a great movie, Alan Rickman at his best! And yes it is a christmas movie.

Groundhog Day

A great movie about personal growth, with an original concept (for the time). Bill Murray is always a treat.

Baby Driver

A great action movie with style and charisma oozing out of every frame. Edgar Wright is a genius!
